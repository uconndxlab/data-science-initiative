What is it?

For a variety of reasons we believe that Stamford is poised to become the next major tech hub, and we’re here to help plant some seeds. The initiative is a partnership between UConn, CTNext, and StamfordNext that builds on numerous successes in Stamford to enhance the existing entrepreneurial and research ecosystem around data science.
