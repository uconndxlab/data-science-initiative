Hackathon

Whether you are just learning about crypto or have been a fan for years, join us for a 2-day Virtual Hackathon to participate in games, challenges and compete for $10,000 in prizes. You’ll also have the opportunity to interview for open jobs with our sponsors.

What is it?

Virtual Crypto Hackathon. A Hackathon is an event where coders and innovators use data to solve problems and create new business opportunities.

Problem statement: Join CoinDesk and TradeBlock to put your Data Science knowledge to work by identifying patterns in Cryptocurrency market data, detecting potentially fraudulent Blockchain transactions, and visualizing multidimensional time series data. For more information, click here.

When: February 17-19, 2022.

Who: UConn students of all grade levels and those familiar with coding from various majors are invited to compete.

Schedule & Prizes: Click to view

Application Deadline: February 10, 2022

FAQs

There will be plenty of instructions provided if you are not familiar with blockchains and bitcoin. In addition, there will be Q&A sessions with professors and industry experts.

Multiple data science techniques could be useful in solving the main problem including Statistical Machine Learning methods as well as Deep Learning models.

This is similar to a Kaggle competition in its problem statement and quantitative analysis of results, but very different in its interactive nature and focus on learning about blockchains and job opportunities within the industry.

All lunch & learns and other events are optional, but you don't want to miss out on any surprise giveaways.

Reach out to Rainer Nunez (rainer@tradeblock.com) for more information.

TradeBlock

TradeBlock's mission is to be the market leading institutional crypto platform that streamlines liquidity access, trading workflows, reduces costs and enables regulatory compliance for market participants, through standardized connectivity to key infrastructure and service providers.

CoinDesk Indices

CoinDesk Indices are the industry standard for institutional single- and multi-asset benchmarks for crypto assets. The flagship CoinDesk Bitcoin Price Index (XBX) has been in operation continuously since 2014. Financial institutions rely on the XBX and other CoinDesk Indices products to benchmark billions of dollars in assets under management and to price hundreds of millions in over-the-counter transactions, daily.
