Where?

The Stamford Data Science Initiative is physically based in a 5600 square foot suite at 9 West Broad Street in Stamford (directly across Washington Street from the main UConn Stamford Campus). We’re also currently renovating a 1200 square foot 5G Innovation Lab on the main UConn Stamford campus where the Stamford Startup Studio will primarily be housed.
