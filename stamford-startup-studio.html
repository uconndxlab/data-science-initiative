Stamford Startup Studio (S3)

The Stamford Startup Studio is a two-semester entrepreneurial working co-op experience for undergraduates, which will run from fall 2021 through spring 2022 and is a program of the Werth Institute for Entrepreneurship and Innovation.

Eight of UConn’s most talented and motivated students will comprise the initial cohort (see below) and will be working together to create technologies & products for the real estate and construction industry.

Throughout the cooperative educational experience, students will gain competencies in the entrepreneurial mindset, project management, design, and engineering.
